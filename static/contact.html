Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? +
Use the Track Order page with your order ID. You also get a tracking email once your order ships.
What is your return policy? +
14-day hassle-free returns. Items must be unworn with original tags. Visit our Returns page to initiate.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard: 3–5 business days (Free above ₹999). Express: 1–2 days (₹149). Same-day in Delhi/NCR for orders before 12 PM (₹299).
Is Cash on Delivery available? +
Yes! COD available for orders up to ₹10,000 with a ₹49 convenience fee.
How do I use a coupon code? +
Enter your code on the cart page before checkout. Valid codes: DRIP20, FLAT200, FIRST10, WELCOME.
